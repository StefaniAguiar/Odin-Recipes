Chicken-Okra stew recipe

Description

This delicious African ingredient combined with chicken will make you show your best cook version.

Ingredients

  • Okra
  • 1 Habanero Pepper
  • 2 pounds skinless, boneless chicken
  • 1 chopped onion
  • 2/3 cup finely chopped celery
  • 2 1/2 cups chopped plum tomatos
  • 2 tablespoon parsley
  • 3/4 teaspoon salt
  • 1 teaspoon ground black pepper

Steps

  1. Heat two teaspoon oil in a Dutch oven over medium-high heat. Add half of chicken to pan.
  2. Cook for 6 minutes, browning on all sides.
  3. Remove the chicken from the pan. Add the remaining chicken to the pan.
  4. Cook for 6 minutes, browning all sides.
  5. Remove the chicken from the pan.
